History


The Alpha Beta chapter was originally established at the University of Michigan on April 4th, 1925; but due to the Great depression, our chapter was closed. We were then reestablished in the late 60's only to be closed once again due to the war in Vietnam. We were most recently recolonized on March 23rd, 2007 and then reestablished as a chapter November 8th, 2008.


Nationally, Sigma Pi was founded on Februrary 26th, 1897 by Rolin Roscoe James, William Raper Kennedy, James Thomspson Kingsbury, and George Martin Patterson. For a full national history, Click Here.
